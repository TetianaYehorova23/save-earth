Ways to help

Protecting the planet means opposing the destruction of the oceans, forests and climate and fighting to defend our air, land and waters from wild pollution. Solutions, changes, actions are needed. We need people like you by our side!

Oceans

The ocean produces 50% of the oxygen on earth, it is a Blue Planet full of life, in whose depths still hidden treasures are hidden. Unfortunately, our seas are collapsing: emptied and violated by industrial fishing, plastic pollution, oil drilling, climate change. Together we can expect a network of marine sanctuaries, which allow the ecosystem to thrive.

Climate

Tackling the climate emergency is the greatest challenge of our times. It must be done today, or there will be no tomorrow! Climate change is already impacting the present: marine ecosystems, primary forests, people's lives and the survival of species, with devastating consequences on biodiversity, on the food we eat, on our future and that of the next generations.

Forest

80% of forest destruction is caused by the intensive production of raw materials such as soy, palm oil, meat. Forests are not a discount store, but the home of unique animals that risk extinction and Indigenous Peoples who fight to defend them. To save the climate and biodiversity, we must save forests.
